Cyclone shelter access & storm-surge vulnerability

Khontakata Union, Sarankhola Upazila, Bagerhat

A full spatial vulnerability study of a coastal union on the edge of the Sundarbans: terrain elevation modelling, storm-surge inundation scenarios at 4 m and 6 m, physical and building vulnerability indices (PVI/BVI), and cyclone shelter service-area analysis identifying served, unserved and overserved communities.
