01 — About

Planning with evidence,
from the ward level up

I'm an urban and regional planner (B.URP, KUET) with 6+ years across NGOs and consultancies, currently coordinating Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning for GO4IMPact — a WaterAid–Swisscontact consortium programme funded by the Embassy of Switzerland — at Rupantar.

My work sits where field reality meets data: designing MEL frameworks and survey tools, running data collection with ODK and KoboToolbox, analysing results in SPSS, and turning it all into maps, dashboards and reports that stakeholders actually use — from union parishads to international donors.

Before this I worked as an urban planner on feasibility studies and GIS mapping projects for Dhaka's city corporations, the Rural Electrification Board, Bangladesh Red Crescent Society and the Urban Development Directorate — including drone surveys for the Bangladesh Coast Guard.
